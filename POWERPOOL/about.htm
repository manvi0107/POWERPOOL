Who We Are?

At PowerPool, our mission is to accelerate the global shift towards renewable energy. We believe in providing innovative platforms that bridge the gap between professionals, consumers, and renewable energy solutions.

Our Mission

Our goal is to empower individuals, businesses, and communities by connecting them to renewable energy opportunities. Whether you're looking for a career in the green sector, a way to share stored energy, or sustainable solutions for disaster relief, we are here to help.

Our Key Initiatives

Renewable Energy Job Finder

Our Renewable Energy Job Finder connects professionals and students with internships, full-time roles, and volunteer opportunities in the green energy sector. By fostering a skilled workforce, we help individuals transition into sustainable careers, supporting the global shift towards renewable energy and a cleaner future.
We promote careers in renewable energy and help people transition into green jobs.

Energy Storage Marketplace

The Energy Storage Marketplace enables individuals with home energy storage systems, such as solar batteries, to trade or sell excess renewable energy within their communities. This decentralized platform promotes energy sharing, reduces reliance on traditional grids, and empowers communities to take control of their renewable energy resources.
We facilitate the sharing of renewable energy resources at a local level.

Sustainable Energy for Disaster Relief

Our Sustainable Energy for Disaster Relief initiative deploys portable renewable energy solutions, like solar and wind units, to disaster-stricken areas. These solutions provide essential power for relief operations, ensuring that renewable energy can support critical needs in emergencies while minimizing the environmental impact of recovery efforts.
We ensure that renewable energy can be used effectively in emergency situations.
